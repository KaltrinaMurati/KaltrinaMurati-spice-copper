About us

Cooper&Spice brings a blend of rich history and modern elegance to every dining experience. Originally founded in 1986 as a cozy gathering spot for locals, it has grown into a beloved destination known for its warm ambiance and timeless charm. Our interiors feature soft lighting, carefully curated decor that reflect our roots and celebrate the beauty of simplicity.
